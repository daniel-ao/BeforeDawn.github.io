Groupe I

This project is mainly a teamwork project because we all work together and split our work to be more efficient. So, this project can teach us how to manage our time as a group as much as how important it is to redistribute all tasks. Communication is an essential factor to carry out our project. As we come from different sections, we must overcome the language barrier by speaking English. Making games gives us a better state of mind, logic, aesthetics, experience, sense of achievement, and a bigger picture. In fact, what we would like to do is, of course, to develop a game independently to our satisfaction. If we develop independently, the necessary planning and design management can exercise our big picture, while the bugs we encounter in the process of making the game and the difficulties we encounter in the learning process will allow us to help each other, asking questions on everything.
