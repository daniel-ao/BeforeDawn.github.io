What is "BeforeDawn" ?

”Before Dawn” is a Tower Defense game, but there are some different. It is a real-time strategy video game played by building turrets or similar buildings on the map to stop the game’s enemies from reaching the fortress. BeforeDawn is an exciting and thrilling game that will keep you on the edge of your seat. The game is reminiscent of the popular games Clash Royale and WarCraftIII but ,the game has become different to a classic tower defense game; it is no longer a simple defensive computer attack; the game now requires the player to train his troops and destroy the enemy's main base through a robust military force to win. The whole game gets harder but also more exciting.


How to play ?


This is an RTS game where the game aims to destroy the main base of the opposing enemy. Each player has their own territory.
Since enemy home bases do not explode on their own, we need to train some soldiers to attack and destroy the enemy home base. Soldiers do not grow out of the ground by themselves, so we need to build our own training camps to train our soldiers. Of course, training soldiers isn't free, it costs money, so we need to spend in-game 'gold' to train soldiers, and gold doesn't come from magic; we need to dig it up in the gold mines.

Once the gold is mined, we can do many things, train soldiers, and build defense towers to protect our base.

you must mine gold and build defenses to protect your base. The game revolves around mining gold and using it to build defenses. You start with a limited amount of gold, but as you progress, you can mine more gold and build more elaborate defenses. You can construct walls, turrets, traps, and other defenses that will help protect your base from enemy attacks. The enemy player is doing the same thing, mining gold and building defenses to protect their base. As the game progresses, you must balance the need to mine gold with the need to build defenses. You must also keep an eye on your enemy's base and try to find weaknesses in their defenses.

The game is as simple as that and very easy to understand.

History


“Before Dawn” is a game that immerses the player in a futuristic and fantasy world. An evil force of darkness has invaded a beautiful world with an elf, fae, humans, and dwarfs who lived together and whenever darkness comes, they turn into monsters and invade the entire country. But a few people survived and to defend themself, they decided to create robots to face monsters. You have been tasked with stopping the enemy’s advance, defending yourself from the invasion before the dawn comes. And your task is to set up an ambush on the path of the monsters or robots and prevent them from entering the camp.
